Automated Expiry Date Tracking System

Automated Expiry Date Tracking System is a revolutionary project aimed at reducing food waste and enhancing inventory management. This system integrates barcode scanners with machine learning to streamline the tracking of product expiry dates.

Key features include:

  • Barcode scanning to capture product information quickly and accurately.
  • Machine learning algorithms to predict expiry trends and provide personalized suggestions.
  • Automated reminders to notify users of nearing expiry dates.
  • Recipe suggestions to utilize products before they expire, minimizing waste.

This project empowers households and businesses to adopt sustainable practices by ensuring timely usage of perishable items, thereby contributing to environmental conservation.
